Smart Hotels

Analyzing customer reviews to suggest hotel improvements

This app will help hotel managers find the topics discussed by its customers in their reviews. It helps them save valuable time by not having to read through thousands of reviews written by previous customers. At the same time this web application is capable of finding out the sentiment score of each sentence in a review and compare topic sentiments with other hotels.
